Instructions

  • Click on squares in the grid to uncover what's underneath.
  • Avoid clicking any bombs! The number in a square tells you how many of the 8 surrounding squares contain a bomb.
  • Hold Shift and click to mark the location of bombs, hold Shift and click to remove a marker.
  • In real life, landmines hurt and kill people. After enjoying the strategy game, learn more about what you can do to to help. International Campaign to Ban Landmines is a good place to start.
