Instructions

  • Click on squares in the grid to uncover what's underneath
  • Avoid clicking any bombs! The numbers tell you how many of the 8 surrounding squares contain a bomb
  • Hold Shift and click to mark the location of bombs, hold Shift and click to remove a marker
  • Land mines are horrific. Enjoy playing this strategy game, and then learn more about what you can do to. Here is just one organization that is working to end unexploded ordnance proliferation.
